About This Tool

The Onboarding Assistant is an AI-powered field companion built for the Awesome Customer Onboarding program. It combines two sources of intelligence — the official SOP and live school data — to give every field employee a quick, specific, actionable answer without reading through documents or pulling up spreadsheets mid-conversation.

What It Can Do
SOP Queries

Ask about process, timelines, escalation steps, or roles. The tool answers from the SOP and cites the exact section.

What is the Red threshold for hardware?
Who owns the Parent Orientation?
What evidence is needed for a milestone audit?
Portfolio View

Select your role and name. Ask about your portfolio and the tool shows only your assigned schools with live RAG status.

Show me my school portfolio
Which of my schools need immediate attention?
School Deep Dive

Select a school from the search bar. Every question is automatically scoped to that school.

What is the current RAG status?
What are the next steps for this school?
Aggregate Analysis

Ask questions across all schools or your portfolio.

How many schools have GAC/APM pending?
List schools where Parent Orientation is overdue
How to Use It
1Select your role from the top right dropdown.
2Select your name from the employee dropdown (FTM and CSM only). Choose Overall to see all schools.
3Optionally select a school from the search box to scope all questions to that school.
4Ask your question naturally. The tool understands context.
5Every answer ends with a source citation — SOP section or School Database.
Best Practices
  • Always select your employee name before asking portfolio questions.
  • Select a school from the dropdown before asking school-specific questions.
  • Start a New Conversation when switching between schools or topics.
  • If the answer seems incomplete, add more context — mention the milestone name or school stage.
  • The tool only answers from the SOP and your school database — it will not guess.
